Industrial spaces in Dubai, known for their fast-paced growth and varied operational demands, require robust, functional, and tailored solutions to optimize workflow and ensure safety. Among these solutions, double hinged doors stand out as a versatile choice. Whether it’s a pharmaceutical facility, a logistics hub, or a food processing plant, customizing double hinged doors to meet specific industrial needs is crucial for achieving efficiency and compliance.
In this blog, we’ll explore the importance of customized double hinged doors in Dubai’s industrial landscape, discuss various customization options, and explain how Swiza Safetech LLP can provide high-quality, tailored solutions.

Why Double Hinged Doors Are Popular in Dubai’s Industries

Double hinged doors are a preferred choice in industrial settings for several reasons:



  • Space Efficiency: They allow for wide openings, accommodating large machinery, equipment, and high traffic.
  • Durability: Built to withstand harsh conditions and heavy usage.
  • Versatility: Suitable for various industrial applications, from warehouses to laboratories.
  • Safety and Compliance: Designed to meet stringent safety and regulatory requirements.

In Dubai’s diverse industrial sectors, these doors offer more than basic functionality. Customization ensures they align perfectly with operational needs, enhancing productivity and safety.

Customization Options for Double Hinged Doors

1. Material Selection

Different industries require doors made from specific materials to meet functional and regulatory demands.

  • Steel Doors:: Ideal for heavy-duty applications in warehouses and factories. They offer excellent durability and security.
  • Aluminum Doors: Lightweight yet strong, aluminum doors are perfect for environments where corrosion resistance is key, such as marine industries.
  • Stainless Steel Doors: Often used in pharmaceutical and food processing industries due to their hygiene-friendly properties.
  • PVC-Coated Doors: Common in cleanrooms, these doors resist dust and contamination.

Swiza Safetech LLP specializes in offering material options tailored to the unique demands of Dubai’s industries.

3. Size and Dimensions

Industrial operations often require doors in non-standard sizes to accommodate large machinery or equipment. Swiza Safetech LLP offers doors in customizable dimensions to ensure a perfect fit for any facility, ensuring operational fluidity.

4. Access Control and Automation

Adding automation and access control systems to double hinged doors enhances security and convenience. Popular options include:

  • Sensor-Activated Doors: Ideal for high-traffic areas, ensuring smooth and contactless entry.
  • Access Card Readers : Providing restricted access for enhanced security in sensitive zones.
  • Emergency Release Mechanisms: Essential for safety compliance, especially in hazardous environments.

Swiza Safetech LLP integrates smart access solutions into its double hinged doors, making them future-ready for Dubai’s technologically advanced industries.

5. Aesthetic Customization

While functionality takes precedence, the visual appeal of industrial spaces cannot be ignored. Custom options include:

  • Color Matching: Doors can be powder-coated or painted to match the brand identity of the business.
  • Textured Finishes: Adding a modern look to industrial interiors.
  • Glass Panels: For visibility and natural light, particularly in office spaces within industrial facilities.

Applications of Custom Double Hinged Doors in Dubai



1. Warehouses and Distribution Centers

Factories and workshops can be noisy environments. Double hinged doors in warehouses are essential for efficient operations. Customization ensures they can handle:

  • High Traffic: Withstanding the constant movement of goods, forklifts, and workers.
  • Temperature Control : Insulated doors for cold storage areas.
  • Dust Resistance : Tight seals to prevent contamination of stored items.

2. Pharmaceutical and Healthcare Facilities

In cleanrooms and laboratories, doors must meet strict hygiene standards. Swiza Safetech LLP provides:

  • Antimicrobial Coatings: Preventing the growth of bacteria.
  • Hermetic Seals : Ensuring airtight conditions in sterile environments.
  • Smooth Surfaces : For easy cleaning and maintenance.

3. Food Processing Plants

Food safety regulations demand doors that can prevent contamination and maintain optimal conditions. Customizations include:

  • Waterproof Materials: Ensuring doors withstand frequent cleaning.
  • Temperature Insulation : Maintaining cooling in refrigerated sections.
  • Non-Toxic Coatings: Meeting food safety standards

4. Manufacturing Units

Manufacturers require durable doors that can endure heavy machinery movement and high impact. Features like reinforced hinges and fire-resistant panels are vital in such environments.

5. Hospitality and Retail

In spaces like hotels and large retail outlets, aesthetic and functional needs merge. Double hinged doors can be customized to:

  • Enhance Accessibility: Wide openings for smooth customer flow.
  • Match Interiors : Doors can be designed to complement the decor.
  • Incorporate Branding : With company logos or signature colors.

Benefits of Customizing Double Hinged Doors



  • Enhanced Efficiency: Tailored designs improve workflow and reduce bottlenecks
  • Increased Durability Materials and features are chosen to suit specific operational demands, ensuring longer lifespans.
  • Cost Savings Energy-efficient and durable doors minimize maintenance and operational costs.
  • Compliance with Regulations Customizations ensure adherence to local safety and industrial standards in Dubai.
