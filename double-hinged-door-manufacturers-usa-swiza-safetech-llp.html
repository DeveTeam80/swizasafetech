What Are Double Hinged Doors?


A double hinged door consists of two door panels mounted on hinges, allowing them to swing open from the center. Unlike single doors, double hinged doors offer a grander entryway and greater versatility in operation. These doors can open inwards, outwards, or both directions, depending on their design and installation.

Key Features of Double Hinged Doors



1.Dual Hinges for Enhanced Functionality:

Double hinged doors have hinges on either side of the frame, offering smooth and reliable movement.


2.Wide Openings:

Their design provides a wider passage, ideal for high-traffic areas or spaces requiring easy accessibility.


3.Customizable Design:

Maintenance Tips for Double Hinged Doors



To keep your double hinged doors in top condition, follow these maintenance tips:

1.Clean Regularly:
