What is Welded Wire Mesh Fencing?

Welded wire mesh fencing is made by welding horizontal and vertical wires together at each intersection, creating a sturdy mesh pattern. This type of fence is designed for high durability and security, making it a popular choice in industrial, commercial, and residential applications. The wire mesh is typically made from steel, galvanized for added strength and corrosion resistance. The fence can also be coated in PVC or powder-coated to enhance its appearance and weatherproofing.

Due to its exceptional structural integrity, welded wire mesh is commonly used in high-security areas, where a physical barrier is needed to prevent unauthorized access, or to safeguard assets from theft or vandalism. Moreover, welded wire mesh fences are highly customizable to suit the specific requirements of any industrial or commercial facility.

Applications of Welded Wire Mesh Fencing in the Industrial Sector

The industrial sector benefits greatly from welded wire mesh fencing. Some common applications include:

  • Perimeter Security: Protecting industrial parks and manufacturing facilities.
  • Factory and Warehouse Fencing: Securing machinery, raw materials, and finished goods.
  • Construction Sites: Preventing theft and vandalism.
  • Agricultural Fencing: Protecting livestock and crops from predators and unauthorized access.
  • High-Security Areas: Enhanced with features like razor wire or barbed wire.
  • Recreational Spaces: Used in sports fields and playgrounds for safety.
