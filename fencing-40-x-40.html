Installation and Use


The procedure to install the Swiza Fencing 40 x 40 Column Series is quite straightforward but calls for details so that the installation remains safe and long-lasting.
Planning: Start by planning out your layout for fencing. Measure the perimeter and mark where each column is installed. Level the ground, free of any debris and lawn/grass.
Column Installation: Use high quality anchor fasteners to grout the columns in their positions.
Installation of Mesh Panels: Attaching mesh panels to columns, fastening them with clamps or fasteners. All fasteners should be tightened properly for the fencing to remain stable.
Final Check: After installing the panels, the entire length of the fence should be checked for any problems. Make adjustments as necessary to ensure everything is locked in properly and stable.
