Product Description

The field of robotics is expanding, and this tendency is anticipated to continue. Robotics has been incorporated into the workflow of numerous industries, including the military, industrial, automotive, healthcare, and many more. That does not, however, imply that the employment of robotics is without its own unique requirements. For safety reasons, a robotics cell, for instance, needs to be adequately walled in order to prevent accidents or equipment damage.

As an important safety requirement

ISO-compliance special Fencing

is needed for Robots in case of any malfunctioning. It helps protect the operator of an industrial robot from any injury. A safety fence system is used in automation technology, installed around a handling system or a material flow system. They act as a physical barrier between the robot and employees on the floor. Benefits Of Installing Robotic Fencing Cell

  • The fencing creates a physical barrier between humans and active robotic machinery to minimize risk.
  • Fencing prevents unauthorized access to robotics cells, reducing the chances of accidents.
  • Fencing with appropriate visibility features allows workers to monitor robotic processes closely.
  • Increase the lifespan of the robotic machinery equipment.
  • The cost of installing fencing is outweighed by the potential savings from reduced accidents, injuries, and equipment damage.
  • Increase the worker's productivity with all safety measures installed.
