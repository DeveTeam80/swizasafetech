What Are Single Hinged Doors?

Single-hinged doors are doors mounted on hinges attached to a single side of the door frame. They open and close in a specific direction, either inward or outward, providing controlled access. In industries, these doors are typically crafted from durable materials like metal, wood, or composite materials to meet rigorous demands.

Key Features of Industrial Single Hinged Doors



Industrial single-hinged doors are built with features tailored to meet the specific needs of the environment:

  • 1. Durable Materials: Designed to withstand heavy usage and environmental factors.
  • 2. Fire Resistance: Certain models are designed to contain or prevent the spread of fire.
  • 3. Thermal and Acoustic Insulation: Some hinged doors help in maintaining temperature and reducing noise.
  • 4. Safety Mechanisms: Equipped with safety locks, alarms, or sensors for secure operation.
  • 5. Ease of Maintenance: Built for frequent use, they require minimal upkeep.
